The Beatles

Who are they?

The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960. They are regarded as the most influential band of all time and were integral to the development of 1960s counterculture and the recognition of popular music as an art form. Here are the 4 members who are John Lennon, the lead singer and guitarist, Paul McCartney, the bass player, George Harrison, guitarist, and Ringo Starr, the drummer. All four of the band members were born and raised in the Liverpool area of England. The Beatles are closely associated with Liverpool and the city has a special place in the history of popular music. They are known for their innovative songwriting, vocal harmonies, and groundbreaking recordings, as well as for their iconic album covers and innovative music videos.
