Ramin Karimloo

What he is known for:

Ramin is a Canadian actor, singer and songwriter recognized for his work in London's West End and New York's Broadway theatre. He's also performed show tunes on a series of solo recordings, including his second album, 2019's From Now On, which landed on the Billboard Heatseeker's chart. Here are a few roles that he's played in throughout the years:

Raoul, Vicomte de  Changy 

(2003-2004)

 

The Phantom  (Love Never Dies)

 (2008, 2010-2011)

The Phantom (25th Anniversary of The Phantom of the Opera) 

 (2011)

Gleb (Anastasia)

(2017) 

Jean Valjean (Les Mis)

 (2011-2015)
